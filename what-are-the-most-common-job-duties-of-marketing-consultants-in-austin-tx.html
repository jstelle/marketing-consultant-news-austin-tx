Marketing consultants are business consultants who specialize in marketing. They help companies create and implement marketing strategies that will lead to more sales. These include things like creating marketing campaigns, writing marketing emails and social media posts, and attending marketing events. Marketing consultants may also advise clients on how to use technology to improve their marketing efforts. Some marketing consultants specialize in a particular industry or type of company. Others are generalists who can work with any type of business. Regardless of specialty, marketing consultants all have the same basic responsibilities during the hiring process and once hired. These include communicating regularly with their clients, keeping them informed about progress and issues, and helping them evaluate the effectiveness of their marketing efforts.
